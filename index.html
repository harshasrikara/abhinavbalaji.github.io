Abhinav - Master of camera executes the dream of his vision by capturing stills of ephemeral moments before they vanish. Growing up in Bangalore, India he witnessed scenes of beauty that simply had to be remembered. Flowers in bloom, a family asleep, sparkle lights that only shine for seconds. These are things that cannot be described. They have to be seen before being lost to the passage of time. A wilted flower, a raging animal, dry ashes. They carry no passion once their moment has passed.
